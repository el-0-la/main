April 2025

Brief thoughts:

This month has gone by faster than expected.
I lost a week to plumbing issues and the stress of dealing with said issues.
My pottery class is coming to an end and I will miss it greatly—most of what I created was wonky but I had a good time and look forward to enrolling in another class in the future.
With help from my dad, the car now has a bed in the back for future adventures. Once I have my set up a little more formalized, I'll be sharing the details.
For the month of March and April, I have been writing a zine on Brain Rot. I'm still writing it. My goal was to release a zine a month, but I've since reassessed that goal and decided on aiming for 4 zines this year which feels more realistic given my pacing—that said, if I feel like I can do more, I will!
